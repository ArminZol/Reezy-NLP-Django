    Who is Reezy for?

    Short answer - students! Long answer - we are dedicated to helping kids that are in school and have trouble with the English language, but are eager to learn it

    How Can it Help?

    With Reezy's powerful simplification platform, students learn English faster by understanding sentences with complex lingo, as well as retaining the lingo in their vocabulary

    info_outline

    How Does it Simplify?

    We replace each hard/uncommon/long word with the most closely related, and simplest word we find based on the students individual vocabulary. For example, the word "idiosyncrasy" will be replaced with "habit"

    A modern way of helping expand the way students learn

    list

    How Does it Work?
    • First we break down each sentence in words
    • Then we go through each word to check if it classifies as "hard"
    • Next we run each hard word through our ranking algorithms that decided how to simplifiy it
    • And finally the result is shown to you!
